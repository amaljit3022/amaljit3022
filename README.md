## Engineering × Software

I am a **Civil Engineer working in public water infrastructure in Assam, India**. I build software, geospatial workflows, and AI-assisted systems that help engineers organise data, analyse infrastructure, automate repetitive work, and make better technical decisions.

My work sits at the intersection of:

* **Water engineering** — pressurised supply, 24×7 systems, hydraulic modelling, and source sustainability
* **Geospatial systems** — GIS, remote sensing, field data, maps, and spatial databases
* **Digital infrastructure** — IoT, SCADA, asset information, dashboards, and digital twins
* **AI automation** — RAG systems, agentic workflows, document intelligence, and engineering knowledge tools

> **Current direction:** turning fragmented engineering data, models, documents, and field observations into connected, reusable systems.
